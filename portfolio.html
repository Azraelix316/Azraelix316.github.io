MCM: 2025

Designed and created a minimax model to optimize a tax on tourism in Juneau, Alaska, for problem B of the Mathematical Competition in Modeling, an undergraduate and high school competition. I participated in a team of 3 freshmen, and we won the Honorable Mention award for our solution (top ~31% of all teams). The model was created in C++ and the final 24-page paper was written in LaTeX, all in under 3 days. During this experience, I developed my C++ skills and my mathematical modeling ability.
