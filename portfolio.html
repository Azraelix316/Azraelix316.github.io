MCM

The Mathematical Competition in Modeling (MCM) is a math modeling competition for high school and undergraduate students involving real-world problems. In 2025, I participated as a freshman in a team of 3 and won an Honorable Mention ranking for problem B (top ~31% of all submissions), which was to model the stresses and potential solutions of overtourism in Juneau, Alaska. The resulting minimax model and paper required us to conduct extensive research, implement a model design, and write a high-quality 25 page paper in 3 days.
